How It Works

Spenderly is a 100% client-side application. This means all the data you enter stays right in your browser. You can add participants, create expense categories, and input values with ease. The interactive table and dynamic chart update in real-time, giving you a clear overview of who owes what. When you're done, you can download a professional-looking PDF report of the entire breakdown.
